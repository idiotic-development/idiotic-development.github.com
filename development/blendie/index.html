Blendie App

Description

Blendie lets you capture the whole moment.

Blendie uses both front and back cameras to create beautiful double exposures. Blendie allows you to capture the entire moment by blending a picture of you plus the scenery around you. After you create a blend, swipe to view the creative blend modes.

It's simple.
Take a great selfie.
Take an interesting picture.
Swipe to view the different blend modes.
Tap the share button to save the blend.
